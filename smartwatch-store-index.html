Reviews (2,341)

The NOVA S7 is engineered for people who demand more from their everyday carry. With a razor-thin 1.9-inch AMOLED display and a titanium-grade case, it's built to be both beautiful and tough — surviving the boardroom and the trail with equal ease.

At its core is a next-generation health chip that continuously monitors your heart rate, blood oxygen, skin temperature, and stress levels. The advanced sleep tracking algorithm breaks down your rest into REM, light, and deep stages, then gives you a personalised recovery score each morning.

For fitness enthusiasts, the NOVA S7 supports over 100 sport modes — from marathon running and open-water swimming to HIIT and yoga. Dual-band GPS locks onto your position in seconds, delivering route maps and pace data directly to your wrist. With 32GB of onboard storage, your training playlists travel with you.

Stay seamlessly connected with smart notifications, calendar reminders, and contactless payments. Compatible with both iOS and Android, setup takes under 2 minutes via the NOVA app.
